About

Welcome to Franchi seGo

The franchiseGo is a complete franchise solution company, operating in India since 2020. We are working with entrepreneurs & investors and helping them recognize their purpose for investing their time & finances. We have worked tirelessly to match the perfect business opportunity for our clients, whether individuals, couples, groups, or companies. We have well-defined system and strategy that ensure you engage with companies precisely and have a mutually profitable association.  

At FRANCHISEGO, our team aims to develop assets such as food courts, food clusters, lounges and standalone outlets delivering maximum value to real estate owner, investors, brands and ourselves. Through our various companies, we create sustainable businesses by delivering KPI (Key Performance Indictors) leading experiences at every stage of the Food & Beverage business. Using our brand narratives, franchising, manpower, supply chain, and logistics expertise, we can add value to every stage of the food business lifecycle.

FranchiseGO deals in the food and beverage franchises in India, as Indians love to eat and live to eat. If you are looking to franchise in a small town as well, this is a growing opportunity for you. This caters to various brands. Indian food is not only about the spices, it is a culture with a tasty history. Each food has a story to tell. Food made relationships strong lasting. And we make the business of food based on transparency with clients and create the relationship between the brand owner & franchiser that crafts a profitable business model for everyone.

How we perform

We have retailers running from Punjab to Delhi NCR and Udaipur to Bangalore; we have covered the geographies and demographics of India. Our brand Relationships, Food Clusters signed, Food Courts and alliances nowadays supply us element over maximum inside the F&B industry.

Operations

The backend of all operations is manpower. After the lockdown we understood the importance of quality & cleanliness in our food. We figured that trained and skilled manpower with limited rate could be the game changer. This was promptly followed by creation of FranchiseGo Academy with its own curriculum and modules to train a fresh school or college pass out into a skilled staff in the kitchen and backend roles. Today, our curriculum is approved by the Tourism and Hospital Skill Council. We have affiliation with 12 reputed IHM institutes to train our enrolments, and this count is increasing by the fortnight. Our own campus at Noida is facilitated to train a batch of 120 students in one go. And this gives us an endless pool of staff to run our operations effortlessly – right from stewards, to POS managers, store managers, Chefs and the entire team that makes your experience at our food outlets ‘finger licking good’.
